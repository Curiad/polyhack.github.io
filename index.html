#polyhack is a community of programmers based on the IRC freenode network. It is specifically a polyglot group concerned to discuss longterm programming ideas and share knowledge without bias to a single language.

Its membership is predominantly from the Australian east coast but includes members from North America, New Zealand, and further. It was created in August, 2011,
